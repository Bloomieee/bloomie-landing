# landing-bloomie
Bloomie is a digital skincare solution that combines artificial intelligence and human-centered design to help users monitor and understand their skin health over time.
The platform offers visual tracking, personalized notes, and dermatologist-supported insights, fostering healthy skincare habits through accessible and inclusive design.

Authors:

Martin Asmat
Carla Gallardo
Luciana Mechan
Mario Sejuro

Core Functionalities:

- Skin Progress Tracker: daily and weekly visual comparison of key metrics (brightness, hydration, texture, spots).
- AI-assisted skin analysis: detects and visualizes subtle changes over time.
- Notes & Calendar: users can record observations, emotions, or product reactions per day.
- Radar Chart Visualization: shows progress across four metrics with “Today / 1 Week / 1 Month” comparison.}

Target Segments:

1. End Users
Individuals between 18 and 35 years old interested in improving and maintaining their skin health.
Users seeking a visual and structured way to track progress and evaluate routine effectiveness.

2. Certified Dermatologists
Licensed professionals interested in offering remote consultations within Bloomie’s platform.
Dermatologists who seek digital tools to evaluate skin condition through AI-assisted imaging and data history.
